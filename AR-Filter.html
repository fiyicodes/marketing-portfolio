Why Brands need to do more AR Filter Campaigns

  • Category: Brand Strategy
  • Posted By: Fiyifoluwa Onafeso
  • Posted On: 12/11/2020

AR Filter (Augmented Reality) has quickly become a popular trend among most social media channels, with over a billion people already using AR filters to engage with their audience because of its ability to get viral fast if well implemented.

By 2022 it is projected that the total amount of mobile augmented reality users would hit 3.5billion if not more, and that’s about 44% of the world’s population, so what better time to start exploring the endless possibilities and opportunities surrounding AR? AR can be used for different kinds of campaigns just depending on how good the strategy is and how well it is implemented.

The use of AR filter campaign for your brand helps make you stand out from your other competitors it makes your brand unique!.

Having your own AR Filter means you can easily personalize it to suit your brand by representing your brand's values and culture. This will help potential customers get to know more about your brand in a fun interactive way.

The ability for AR Filters to rapidly go viral due to its nature will help to organically increase your brand awareness and recognition and help your brand to get more potential customers who would likely interact with your services. Potential customers who come from your AR filters also have a higher chance of being 100 % loyal to the brand.

The future is AR, and the earlier you start practicing and incorporating it into your brand, the better, you definitely do not want your brand to be left out of the big picture as the endless possibilities of AR will only keep increasing.
