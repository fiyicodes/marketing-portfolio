My Success Story on Growth Hacking

  • Category: Growth Hacking
  • Posted By: Fiyifoluwa Onafeso
  • Posted On: 8/12/2020

I remember in 2017, when I just started falling in Love with technology, I wanted to share my passion for it by creating my own Instagram page where I post the latest news, trends, meme, etc., but I realized that my post was getting very few engagement and interactions, but rather than me quitting I did my research and discovered Digital Marketing, Social Media Marketing, Hashtag Marketing, and Growth Hacking, So I took some courses, watched some tutorials, followed Instagram Algorithm updates, I found this whole process fun, and this was how my passion for Digital Marketing started as I started seeing results, more likes and better engagements.

Fast forward to the beginning of 2020, I had 50K followers, I continued using my strategies to boost my page organically. Now from having no impressions and likes I now have 76K+ followers, 10 million organic impressions weekly, 5K+ plus new followers every month, there were times I wanted to give up and just delete the account because of bad engagements. Still, I kept going because it became something part of me. Growing a page organically takes time, effort, consistency, and perseverance it is not something that happens overnight, but if you keep going long enough, you will see huge and everlasting results. Nothing beats gaining organic followers, they end up becoming a big family, grateful for my 76K family!
