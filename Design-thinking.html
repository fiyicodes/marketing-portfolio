What is Design Thinking ?

  • Category: UX Design
  • Posted By: Fiyifoluwa Onafeso
  • Posted On: 26/11/2020

Design Thinking helps in providing a solution based approach when trying to solve problems, I have shared 5 major steps involved in Design Thinking.

Empathy: You need to know more about your users or target audience by conducting Interviews or Surveys, understanding and sympathizing with your target audience would help you in building an application that your users would love.

Define: After conducting the interviews or surveys you will be able to identify certain problems your user would face while performing certain tasks.

Ideate: When you are able to identify and define the problems your target audience could face, you then try to come up with solutions by sketching out designs that would help solve the problem.

Prototype: After completing your sketches you have to make sure you prototype your sketches that shows details about the flow of your application.

Testing: This is the final stage where real people test your application, you get feedback from them and learn what works best and what doesn't work best. Design thinking is the difference between a successful application and a failed application.
